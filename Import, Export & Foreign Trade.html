What We Offer

Our Import, Export & Foreign Trade Services

IEC Code Registration

Import Export Code registration with DGFT — the mandatory licence for any business engaged in import or export of goods and services.

Customs Compliance

Guidance on Bill of Entry, Shipping Bill, customs duty calculations, HS code classification, and customs clearance procedures.

Export Promotion Schemes

Assistance in claiming benefits under RoDTEP, MEIS, SEIS, Duty Drawback, Advance Authorisation, and EPCG schemes.

DGFT Filings & Licences

All DGFT-related applications including advance authorisation, EPCG licence, RCMC registration, and annual performance updates.

Export Documentation

Preparation and review of commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin, and Letter of Credit documentation.

Foreign Trade Policy Advisory

Expert guidance on FTP 2023 provisions, trade agreements, anti-dumping duties, and customs valuation rules.

What You Need

Documents for IEC Registration

  • PAN Card of the entity (firm/company/individual)
  • Certificate of Incorporation / Partnership Deed
  • Address proof of registered office
  • Cancelled cheque / bank certificate with account details
  • Digital Signature Certificate (for online application)
  • Passport-size photograph of authorised signatory
  • Email ID and mobile number for OTP verification
  • GST Registration Certificate (if applicable)

Note: Our team will provide a precise document checklist tailored to your specific situation after an initial consultation.

Key Advantages

Benefits of Trade Compliance

  • Access to Global Markets — IEC registration opens the door to trade with any country in the world without restriction.
  • Export Incentives — Properly registered exporters can claim significant duty refunds and promotional benefits from the government.
  • No Annual Renewal — IEC is a lifetime registration — no renewal fee or yearly compliance after initial registration.
  • E-Commerce Exports — Mandatory for selling on international e-commerce platforms like Amazon Global, eBay, and Etsy.
  • Foreign Currency Earnings — Export businesses enjoy favourable tax treatment on foreign currency income and FEMA benefits.
  • MSME Export Benefits — Registered MSMEs exporting goods get additional subsidies, insurance, and marketing assistance.
