What We Offer

Our Accounting & Financial Advisory Services

Bookkeeping & Accounts Maintenance

Accurate day-to-day recording of all financial transactions, accounts payable/receivable management, and ledger maintenance.

Accounts Finalization

Preparation of final accounts — trading account, P&L statement, and balance sheet — ready for audit and statutory compliance.

MIS Reporting

Monthly Management Information System reports providing clear dashboards on revenue, costs, profitability, and cash flow for decision-making.

Virtual CFO Services

Part-time CFO advisory for growing businesses — budgeting, financial forecasting, investor relations, and strategic growth planning.

Accounts Payable & Receivable

Streamlined AP/AR management, invoice tracking, vendor payments, debtor follow-ups, and aging analysis.

Financial Planning & Analysis

Business budgeting, variance analysis, profitability analysis, and scenario modelling to support key business decisions.

What You Need

Information We Typically Need

  • Bank statements for all accounts (monthly)
  • Purchase and sales invoices
  • Expense vouchers and receipts
  • Loan statements and interest certificates
  • Payroll details and salary registers
  • Previous year's audited financial statements
  • Details of fixed assets and depreciation schedules
  • Vendor/customer agreements (for contract revenue recognition)

Note: Our team will provide a precise document checklist tailored to your specific situation after an initial consultation.

Key Advantages

Why Good Accounting Drives Growth

  • Real-Time Financial Visibility — Know exactly where your money is, what your profits are, and what your cash position looks like at any point.
  • Better Decision Making — Accurate, up-to-date financial data enables confident decisions on pricing, hiring, investment, and expansion.
  • Audit Readiness — Well-maintained books make statutory audits smooth, fast, and free of surprises or costly adjustments.
  • Tax Efficiency — Proper expense recording and deduction tracking ensures you never overpay taxes or miss eligible claims.
  • Investor & Lender Confidence — Professional financial statements are essential for raising equity investment or obtaining bank financing.
  • Fraud Prevention — Regular reconciliations and internal controls built into the accounting process help detect and prevent fraud early.
