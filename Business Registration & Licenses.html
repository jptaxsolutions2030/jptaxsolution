Business Registration & Licenses

Start your business with the right legal foundation. We handle all registrations and licenses — from company incorporation to trademarks, MSME, and FSSAI.

What We Offer

Our Business Registration & Licenses Services

Company Incorporation (Pvt Ltd / OPC / LLP)

Complete company registration with MCA including name reservation, DIN, DSC, MOA/AOA drafting, and Certificate of Incorporation.

Trademark Registration

Protect your brand with trademark registration under the Trade Marks Act. We handle search, application, examination, and registration.

MSME / Udyam Registration

Register your micro, small, or medium enterprise on the Udyam portal to avail government schemes, subsidies, and priority bank lending.

FSSAI License / Registration

Food Safety and Standards Authority of India registration and license for food businesses — basic, state, and central level.

Shop & Establishment Act

Registration under state-specific Shop & Establishment Act for retail shops, commercial establishments, and service providers.

Other Licenses

Import Export Code (IEC), Professional Tax Registration, Labour License, and all industry-specific registrations managed end-to-end.

What You Need

Common Documents Required

  • PAN & Aadhaar of all directors / partners
  • Passport-size photographs of directors / partners
  • Proof of registered office address (utility bill, rent agreement)
  • Email ID & mobile number for all directors
  • Draft MOA & AOA (we assist in preparation)
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) — we assist
  • Bank account details for the business
  • NOC from property owner (if rented premises)

Note: Our team will provide a precise document checklist tailored to your specific situation after an initial consultation.

Key Advantages

Why Register Your Business

  • Legal Identity — A registered company has a separate legal identity distinct from its owners, limiting personal liability.
  • Access to Credit — Registered businesses find it significantly easier to obtain loans, overdrafts, and investment funding.
  • Brand Protection — Trademark registration prevents others from using your business name, logo, or brand identity.
  • Government Schemes — MSME registration unlocks access to dozens of government subsidies, schemes, and priority sector loans.
  • Build Trust — Customers, vendors, and partners trust registered businesses more, opening more opportunities.
  • Tax Benefits — Incorporated entities enjoy various tax advantages and deductions unavailable to unregistered businesses.
