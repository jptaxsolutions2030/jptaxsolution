Registration Requirements

Documents Required

Business Identity & Address
  • Certificate of Incorporation (for companies)
  • Partnership Deed (for partnerships / LLPs)
  • Proprietor's PAN & Aadhaar (for proprietorships)
  • LLP Agreement (for LLPs)
  • Proof of Business Address (electricity bill / rental agreement)
  • NOC from property owner (if applicable)
Promoter & Other Details
  • Partner / Director photographs
  • Partner / Director PAN & Aadhaar copies
  • Contact information of all partners / directors
  • Authorisation letter (for partnerships / LLPs)
  • Ward number of business address
Key Advantages

Benefits of GST Registration

  • Legal Business Recognition — GST registration gives your business a formal identity and enables legal trade across India.
  • Input Tax Credit (ITC) — Claim credit on taxes paid on purchases, significantly reducing your overall tax burden.
  • Unified Tax System — One tax replaces multiple indirect taxes, simplifying compliance and reducing cascading effects.
  • Pan-India Operations — Registered businesses can trade freely across all states without additional levies.
  • Higher Credibility — GST registration signals legitimacy, building trust with customers, suppliers, and financial institutions.
  • Online & Transparent — Fully digital portal makes compliance easier with clear audit trails and real-time status.

Note: Specific document requirements may vary based on your business structure and state. Our team will guide you through the exact requirements for your situation.
