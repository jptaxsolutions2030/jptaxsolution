What We Offer

Our Services Include

GST Registration

Hassle-free new GST registration for businesses, startups, and individuals crossing the threshold limit, including voluntary and amendment services.

GST Return Filing

Timely filing of all monthly returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) and the annual return (GSTR-9 and 9C) with full accuracy and deadline tracking.

GST Refund Processing

Expert assistance claiming eligible GST refunds for exporters, inverted duty structures, and excess tax payments.

ITC Reconciliation

Meticulous invoice matching between GSTR-2B and purchase records to ensure you claim 100% of eligible Input Tax Credit.

E-Way Bill Management

Generation, modification, and cancellation of E-Way bills for inter-state and intra-state movement of goods above the threshold value.

GST Advisory

Expert guidance on GST applicability, HSN/SAC classification, place of supply rules, and complex industry-specific GST matters.

Notice Response and Litigation

Professional handling of GST notices, replies to department queries, and representation before GST authorities at all levels.

What You Need

Documents for GST Registration

  • Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed, or PAN (based on entity)
  • PAN and Aadhaar of all directors, partners, or proprietor
  • Proof of business address - electricity bill, rent agreement, or NOC
  • Passport-size photographs of authorised signatory
  • Cancelled cheque or bank account statement
  • Ward number of the business premises

Note: Our team provides a tailored checklist after your free consultation.

Key Advantages

Benefits of GST Registration

  • Legal Business Recognition - GST registration gives your business a formal identity enabling legal trade across India.
  • Input Tax Credit - Claim credit on purchases to significantly reduce your overall indirect tax burden.
  • Unified Tax System - One tax replaces multiple indirect taxes, simplifying compliance and eliminating cascading effects.
  • Pan-India Operations - Trade freely across all states without additional levies or local permits.
  • Higher Credibility - Signals legitimacy to customers, suppliers, and financial institutions.
  • Fully Digital - The GST portal provides real-time compliance status and clear audit trails.
